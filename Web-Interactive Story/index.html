Family Comes First

The aim of this game is to know the importance of your family!
Family is everything, but sometimes it can be difficult to put into words just how much your loved ones mean to you. And some people hurts their parents without thinking about their feelings.
Never forget that Family is Where Life begins and Love never ends!

Introduction

Jad a stubborn and bad boy, wasn't good with their parents. He always hurts them without thinking about their feelings.
Once upon a rainy day, when he was searching in the house's basement he found an old and mysterious watch. He wears it and suddenly he goes in time to the next 50 years.

Quotes:

Family is the support you will never have to pay for, they will be there to cheer you on with every of your life goals.

Branches

Branch 1: Start
Jad finds himself in a scary world where he knows nothing. His parents aren’t there and he is alone without money, car, house…
He is hungry but how can he eat?
Jad has two choices:
1- Steal food or money to buy food
2- Find a work so he earns money and buy food

If he choose to work, It will be great! He should also try to earn points by helping people and doing some good things.
If he steal anything, he will pass to the next branch

Branch 2: Jail or run?
If someone stole something, either the police will come and take him to the jail or he should run away. After the stealing, Jad has two choices:
1- Go to the Police office: They will put him in jail then OOPS, GAME OVER!
2- Run away
If he choose to run away he will pass to the next branch or he may return to the branch 1 (Stealing)
He would do another things that're also evil.
He may kill people in order to have money or he may take drugs so he can forget what happened with him.. Many scenarios can happen.

Branch 3: Kill
If kills people he have two choices:
1- Go to the Police office: They will put him in jail then OOPS, GAME OVER!
2- Take drugs to forget what he has done: Next branch
3- Re-kill: return to the beginning of this branch
If he choose to take drugs, he needs money to buy these drugs so he may return to any of the previous branches.

Branch 4: Try to get back to the real world
Now, and after all these bad situations, Jad missed his family. He wants to go back to his real world where all he gets the care and love of his parents.
Suddenly, he finds a quote that say, "Family is the support you will never have to pay for! ALways love you family"
All the bad things he did runs in his head and he started crying. Then he decides to make good things and help people so he return to his home full of love

Branch 5: List of good things
Well Mr. Jad, now you should do the following things: - You should raise awareness to not take drugs and to not kill or steal
- You should help people and make good acts so you compensates your mistakes with your parents and everyone around.
- You should help an old man to pass the road
- You should help a lost kid to find his family
- You should help an NGO in the fundraising for an elderly home
- You should go to the elderly home and get to know each old person and leasten to their stories.
After doing this list, You will get back home and love your parents in the way they deserve. Never hurt them again. Love Them
If You don't do any list option, You will lose and.. GAME OVER

Branch 6: Result
So, based on two variables: Life and time, and of course your choices, You may: 1- Get back to your family: Congrats! 2- GAME OVER!
